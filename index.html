About

Experience that brings structure, clarity, and credibility.

Col Dr Bhasker Gupta (Retd) brings more than three decades of military service, instructional experience, research, and public communication to the training process. His work has included defence education, mentoring, writing, and leadership-focused guidance for aspirants and professionals.

The emphasis here is not on slogans or pressure. It is on helping candidates understand themselves better, improve their expression, strengthen officer-like qualities, and prepare for the SSB with a grounded, disciplined mindset.
